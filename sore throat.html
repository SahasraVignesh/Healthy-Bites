Cure for sore throat

Here's a simple recipe for a soothing Honey Lemon Ginger Tea, known for its comforting properties for a sore throat:

ingredients:

  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Freshly squeezed juice from half a lemon
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, thinly sliced

    instruction:

    • Boil water and let it cool slightly.
    • Add honey, lemon juice, and thinly sliced ginger to the hot water.
    • Allow the mixture to steep for about 5-7 minutes to infuse the flavors.
    • Strain the tea to remove ginger slices if desired.
    • Sip on this warm and soothing tea slowly.

    Feel free to adjust the honey and lemon amounts based on your taste preferences. The combination of honey, lemon, and ginger can provide relief and comfort for a sore throat.
