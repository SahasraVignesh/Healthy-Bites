Cure For Shortness Of Breath

If you're experiencing persistent shortness of breath, it's crucial to consult a healthcare professional promptly. While there's no substitute for professional medical advice, here's a simple breathing exercise that may provide temporary relief:

Deep Breathing Exercise:

  • Sit comfortably or lie down.
  • Inhale slowly and deeply through your nose.
  • Hold your breath for a few seconds.
  • Exhale slowly through pursed lips.
  • Repeat several times.

However, if shortness of breath persists or worsens, seek immediate medical attention. It could be indicative of any serious underlying issue.
