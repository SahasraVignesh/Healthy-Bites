Cure For stomache ache

Certainly! Here's a simple remedie (herbal decoction) for stomach pain:

ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds.
  • 1 teaspoon coriander seeds.
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds.
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, sliced.
  • 1 cup water.

instruction:

  • 1. In a saucepan, combine all the ingredients with one cup of water.
  • 2. Bring the mixture to a boil and then simmer for about 10-15 minutes.
  • 3. Strain the kashayam to remove the solid particles.
  • 4. Allow it to cool to a comfortable temperature.
  • 5. You can add a teaspoon of honey for sweetness, if desired.
