Vegetable and Lentil Soup with Turmeric Why this dish may help with body aches:
  • Lentils: Rich in protein, fiber, and various vitamins and minerals, lentils provide essential nutrients that support overall health and may help reduce inflammation.
  • Turmeric: Contains curcumin, a compound with anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that may help alleviate pain and inflammation associated with body aches.
  • Vegetables: Carrots, celery, onion, kale, and spinach are packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that support immune function and may help reduce inflammation.
  • Ginger: Known for its anti-inflammatory properties, ginger may help reduce muscle soreness and discomfort.
    This vegetable and lentil soup with turmeric is not only comforting and flavourful. Enjoy it as a wholesome meal to help soothe discomfort and support your body's recovery process.

    Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeri
  • 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups chopped kale or spinach
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    Instructions:

    Prepare the Soup:

  • In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add diced onion, carrots, and celery, and cook until softened, about 5-7 minutes.
  • Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and ground turmeric to the pot, and cook for another 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  • Stir in the dried lentils and vegetable broth. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  • Add chopped kale or spinach to the soup and simmer for an additional 5 minutes, until the greens are wilted.
  • Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning as needed.
    Serve: Ladle the vegetable and lentil soup into bowls. Optionally, squeeze fresh lemon juice over each serving and garnish with chopped fresh parsley or cilantro. Serve hot and enjoy!
