Homemade Dish Saves The Day!!

Cure For Nausea

Feel free to adjust the ginger and honey amounts based on your taste preferences.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh ginger (about 1-2 inches), peeled and sliced
  • 2 cups of water
  • Honey (optional)

Instructions:

  • Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a pot.
  • Add the peeled and sliced ginger to the boiling water.
  • Reduce heat and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes to extract the flavor.
  • Strain the tea to remove ginger pieces.
  • Add honey to sweeten if desired.
  • Enjoy the warm ginger tea slowly.
